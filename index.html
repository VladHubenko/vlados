Hutsulshchyna, or Hutsulia, is a Ukrainian ethno-cultural region, the region of Ukrainian Verkhovyna people - Hutsuls, located in Western Ukraine.

Hutsul clothing

Going on a trip here, you enter the world of a truly unusual culture. The Hutsuls, the local inhabitants, are known for their hospitality and devotion to centuries-old traditions. Their outfits, mostly handmade from natural materials, are decorated with bright embroideries and exquisite ornaments that tell the stories of generations.

Hutsul cuisine

Hutsul cuisine is a true masterpiece. Delicious banushes, drumsticks, sausages and Cossack dishes will certainly give you an unforgettable culinary experience. And if you know the locals, they might share with you the secrets of cooking these magical dishes.

The nature of Hutsul

The nature of Hutsul region is a real treasure. Mountain peaks, forests that bloom in autumn with a rainbow of colors, and crystal clear rivers make this place ideal for tourism. If you like active recreation, then mountain ski resorts or cycling routes are waiting for you.

Music and dances

Music and dances: Hutsul music and dances are very popular in Ukraine and abroad. Traditional musical instruments such as the trembita (Hutsul alpine horn) and the bandura are used to create impressive melodies.
